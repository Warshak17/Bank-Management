Online Banking Features

  • Registration is authenticated by our Admins.
  • Transferring amount to another customer is easily done.
  • Customer gets to know his last login date and time.
  • Customer get email notifications whenever he does transaction,withdrawal or credit.
  • Customers can check their last 10 transactions
  • Cutomer can apply for passbook which holds their entire transaction history
  • Customer can request for ATM card and Passbook
  • Admin can add,edit,delete customers and staffs as well
  • Both admin and customer can change their PIN whenever they want.
  • Pin are encrypted for additional safety.
  • Our staffs wont ask you for your pin number. Please dont disclose your Pin Number to anyone
